Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Cowboy Toad Games LTD ("we," "us," or "our") handles your data, how it may be used, and the limited circumstances under which it might be processed when you interact with our games, website, and related services.

1. No Active Data Collection

We design our games with privacy in mind. None of our games actively collect, track, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, IP address, or payment information.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies within our games.
  • We do not transmit gameplay data, preferences, or personal identifiers to our servers or any third party.

2. Limited Data Handling

While our games themselves do not collect data, some limited usage data may be processed through the third-party services we rely on:

  • Cloudflare Analytics: Our website uses Cloudflare Analytics to monitor traffic and performance. This service uses anonymized data to help improve security and site reliability. We do not use Cloudflare to collect personal information or track users across sites.
  • Steam Cloud: If you play our games via Steam, save game data may be stored through Steam Cloud. We do not access or store this data directly. Please refer to the Steam Privacy Policy for more information.

3. Third-Party Platforms

Our games may be downloaded and played through third-party platforms, each of which may collect and process data under their own privacy practices. These platforms include:

  • Steam (Valve Corporation)
  • Apple App Store (Apple Inc.)
  • Google Play Store (Google LLC)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any platform through which you access our games, as we do not control the data collection practices of these services.
