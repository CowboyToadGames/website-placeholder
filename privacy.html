Privacy Policy - Cowboy Toad Games LTD

Our Privacy Policy

Cowboy Toad Games LTD (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data when you interact with our games and services.

No Data Collection

"Nocturnals" does not collect, track, or store any user data. This means:

  • We do not collect personal information, such as names, email addresses, or payment details.
  • We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies within our game.
  • No gameplay data, preferences, or other information is transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Platforms

Nocturnals is distributed through third-party platforms (e.g., Steam). These platforms may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of the platforms where you access our game.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not collect any personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact the platform through which the game was accessed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

As our studio grows and we release new games or services, we may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted here.
